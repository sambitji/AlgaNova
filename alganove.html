Objections

Straight Answers.

No marketing language. The real answers to real concerns.

Will it damage my engine? +
No — under the tested conditions (B20, B50, B100 in 4-stroke petrol and diesel engines). Our 200-hour test showed lower engine wear, not higher. Biofuel has better lubricity than petrol. The only condition: don't mix it with 2-stroke oil-injection systems unless confirmed compatible.
Why is it cheaper than petrol sometimes? +
Algae grows 20–30x faster than land crops. No farmland needed. Production uses wastewater and CO₂ as feedstock — inputs that are free or subsidized. Our current cost is ₹55–65/L to produce. Retail at ₹90 is a margin that funds expansion. Bulk at ₹70 passes more savings to high-volume customers.
How much can you supply? +
Current pilot batch: 1,200 litres. Production capacity: ~400 litres/week at current facility size. We are honest — this is early stage. If you need 10,000 litres/month, we cannot serve you yet. Orders above 500 litres are reviewed individually and batched.
Will mileage drop? +
At B20 blend: no measurable drop. At B100: 3–7% lower mileage vs petrol due to lower energy density. But at ₹90/L vs ₹100/L petrol, cost per km is equal or better. At bulk pricing (₹70), you save money even accounting for the mileage difference.
Is it certified? Can I use it legally? +
Currently in testing phase for BIS IS:15607 certification. ASTM D6751 compliance tests are ongoing. Using it now is at your discretion — it is not yet a government-listed fuel. Vehicle warranties: consult your manufacturer. We recommend B20 blend as the safest starting point during this phase.
What if I try it and don't like it? +
Order the 500ml sample first (₹100 delivered). Run it as a B20 blend. If you see any issue — we refund the sample cost. We'd rather you test and trust than buy and doubt.
